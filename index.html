Hard Money Loan Checklist

A simple guide for beginners and pros to get their loans approved.

General "Must-Haves"

Lenders need these for every loan type.

  • Entity Docs: LLC or Corp papers (most won't lend to people, just companies).
  • ID: A copy of your Driver’s License or Passport.
  • Credit Score: Usually 620 or higher.
  • Proof of Money: 2 months of bank statements to show you have the down payment.

Fix and Flip Checklist

  • Scope of Work (SOW): A list of every repair and what it costs.
  • Contractor Bids: Signed prices from the workers doing the job.
  • Comps: 3-5 nearby houses that sold for your goal price.
  • ADVANCED Investor Resume: A list of all the houses you have flipped before.

Rental / DSCR Checklist

  • Lease Agreement: A copy of the current renter's contract.
  • Rent Roll: A summary showing who pays what in each unit.
  • Appraisal: A pro report showing the "market rent" for the area.
  • ADVANCED 1.20x Ratio: Show that rent is 20% higher than all your bills combined.

Ground Up Construction

  • Blueprints: Full building plans from an architect.
  • Permits: Proof that the city says it is okay to build.
  • Budget Buffer: An extra 10% in cash for "surprises."
  • ADVANCED Builder Resume: The license and history of your General Contractor.

Bridge Loan Checklist

  • Exit Strategy: A clear plan on how you will pay the loan back quickly.
  • Payoff Statement: If you have a loan now, the bank's letter saying how much you owe.
  • Property Photos: Current pictures of the house or building.

Tip: Always check your "Draw Request" rules before you start work!
